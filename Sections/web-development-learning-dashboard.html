What is Web Development

Discover what web development is and how it shapes the digital world we interact with every day.

Learning Objectives

  • Understand the definition and scope of web development
  • Recognize the impact of web development on modern society
  • Identify different types of web development specializations
  • Appreciate the role of developers in building digital experiences

Key Concepts

Web Development: The process of building, creating, and maintaining websites and web applications through coding, programming, and implementation of various technologies.

Frontend Development: Building the visual, interactive parts of websites that users see and interact with directly.

Backend Development: Creating the server-side logic, databases, and APIs that power websites behind the scenes.

Why Web Development Matters

In today's digital age, web development is the foundation of nearly every online interaction. From e-commerce to social media, education to healthcare, skilled developers build the platforms that connect people worldwide.

  • Create accessible digital experiences for billions of users
  • Solve real-world problems through technology
  • Build scalable solutions that impact millions of lives
  • Enable remote work and global collaboration
  • Drive innovation across all industries
