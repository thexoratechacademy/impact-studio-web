What is Product Design

Discover what product design is, its evolution, and how it shapes the digital products we use every day.

Learning Objectives

  • Understand the definition and scope of product design
  • Recognize the evolution of product design in the digital age
  • Identify key principles that guide product designers
  • Appreciate the impact of good design on user experience

Key Concepts

Product Design: The process of creating digital products that solve user problems while meeting business goals. It encompasses both the visual design (UI) and the overall user experience (UX).

Design Thinking: A human-centered approach to innovation that draws from the designer's toolkit to integrate the needs of people, the possibilities of technology, and the requirements for business success.

Why Product Design Matters

In today's digital-first world, product design is the competitive advantage that separates successful products from failures. Great design:

  • Creates intuitive, enjoyable user experiences
  • Solves real user problems effectively
  • Drives business growth and customer loyalty
  • Reduces development costs by catching issues early
  • Builds brand reputation and trust
