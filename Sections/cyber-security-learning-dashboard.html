What is Cybersecurity

Discover what cybersecurity is and why it's critical in today's digital world.

Learning Objectives

  • Understand the definition and scope of cybersecurity
  • Recognize the impact of cyber threats on organizations
  • Identify key cybersecurity principles and frameworks
  • Appreciate the importance of security in modern systems

Key Concepts

Cybersecurity: The practice of protecting computer systems, networks, programs, and data from digital attacks, damage, or unauthorized access.

CIA Triad: The three core principles of information security: Confidentiality (keeping data private), Integrity (ensuring data accuracy), and Availability (ensuring authorized access).

Ethical Hacking: Authorized attempts to gain unauthorized access to computer systems to identify vulnerabilities before malicious hackers can exploit them.

Why Cybersecurity Matters

In our increasingly connected world, cyber threats are growing more sophisticated every day. Organizations need skilled professionals to:

  • Protect sensitive data and privacy
  • Prevent financial losses from cyber attacks
  • Safeguard critical infrastructure
  • Maintain customer trust and reputation
  • Ensure business continuity
